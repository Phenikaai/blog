We are a research group at the Faculty of Computer Science, Phenikaa University. Our aim is to explore applications of AI in different domains. Our research topics including:

  • Applications of AI in communications and networking
  • Applications of AI in Smart Cities
  • Applications of AI in Art
  • Applications of AI in medical and agricultural imaging
  • Applications of AI in new material discovering
